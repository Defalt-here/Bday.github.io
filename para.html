To
my favourite reptile,
I had never ever thought that you'd be as close to me as you are now, frankly the first time I saw you I knew you were way outta my league. Being the pragmatic guy I am I didn't initiate conversation. Though when you came to me asking for those adobe products which you still haven't learnt I knew that you were at best exagerating how surprised you were by my skills or at worst faking it, it is because back then I only recently had a similar experience.

Though it is a complete surprise to me that you infact, were not faking your interest, I was intrigued genuinely. That is when I knew I have a person who'll appreciate me for who I am and not what I am. Since then there haven't been a single day where I haven't flaunted our friendship to people around me and if I don't find people around me I then flaunt it to my own past self!

You have come to be very precious to my life and endeavours in Pune and beyond. I may even say the most precious yet, but I will obviously not say it to your face because I have trouble showing affection (I'd rather code 7 pages of pure HTML CSS and JS than writing you a para and post a good story of yours) but know that it does not mean I don't feel for you. I do, lord knows I do, the heavens know I do.

I care for you and I do get protective too! I fear sometimes if I'll ever be enough for you if I'll ever be as good a friend as you are to me, I have my own doubts and fears about it but I try my best; even through everything I try my best. I'd rather leave LC than lose you (confidential statement) and I really don't know how to show that. I also don't know if I'll be at my fullest capacity when you'll need me. I know you can handle (most cases) on your own but alas I wouldn't want you to be alone at your best or at your worst.

You've been kinder to me claiming you're not than most have been harsh claiming they are kind. You have tended my wounds and tolerated me when I've become intolerable. I am grateful and indebted to your patience your kindness and your unending love. You taught me that I deserve to be happy. You taught me that I am not a mere project, you taught me to be human.

When everyone clapped at my achievements and celebrated my victories, you asked me if I was alright. You loved me for being a human not a robot. For a world filled with LCs and LLM projects and internships you accepted me for simply being, me. There is not a greater love that I know of(Rishi should really be jealous of me). I look up to you, with utmost respect and an even grander admiration and I will for the life of me never forget what you've become to me in such small a time. I wish to have someone with your wits and hearts around me for the rest of my life (someone who's not a jain preferably) and it is in my heart that I tell you, I'll forever fight for you, and with you, up until the gods grow tired of our antics I then plan to fight god.

Forever yours
Aditya.
